StrumForge

-

StrumForge is a simple practice tool for guitar players.

Key features:

  • Generate over 425,000 unique chord progressions instantly
  • Play along with visual chord diagrams in tempo
  • Solo over each progression with built-in mode and pentatonic scale diagrams
  • Lock the key or randomize it
  • Choose one or multiple scales and cycle through them for varied chord progressions
  • Choose open chords, barre chords, triads, or power chords
  • Add color with sevenths, suspended, and add9 chords
  • Lock or manually override individual chords in a progression
  • Chord interval and shape labels
  • Cycle through different shapes and fingerings for the same chord
  • Move chord diagrams to your preferred position
  • Built-in drum groove and tempo control to play along with
  • Four different guitar tones for chord playback
  • Landscape mode for easier progression tracking
  • Left-handed chord diagram support
  • Reset button to clear all settings and start fresh
